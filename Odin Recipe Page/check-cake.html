Checkerboard Cake

This checkerboard cake consists of tender vanilla and chocolate cakes with a rich, creamy chocolate frosting.

Prep Time:

45 mins

Cook Time:

20 mins

Servings:

12

Yield:

1 9-inch layer cake

Total time:

2 hrs 30 mins

Ingredients

Frosting:

  • ½ cup salted butter, softened
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (32 ounce) package powdered sugar
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 4 tablespoons whole milk, or more as needed
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package devil's food cake mix (such as Duncan Hines®)
  • 2 cups whole milk, divided
  • ⅔ cup salted butter, melted, divided
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, divided
  • Baking spray with flour
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package white cake mix (such as Duncan Hines®)
  • 3 large egg whites

Directions

Step 1:

Prepare cakes: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) with racks in middle and lower third positions.

Step 2:

Place devil's food cake mix, 1 cup milk, 1/3 cup melted butter, 3 large eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract in the bowl of a stand mixer. Beat at low speed, using the paddle attachment, for 1 minute until combined. Increase speed to medium and beat for 2 minutes.

Step 3:

Spoon batter evenly into 2 (9-inch) round nonstick cake pans, greased with baking spray; set aside.

Step 4:

Repeat procedure with the white cake mix, substituting 3 egg whites for 3 large eggs.

Step 5:

Bake 4 cakes in the preheated oven until a wooden pick inserted in middle comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes, rotating cake pans from front to back and between top and bottom racks halfway through baking time.

Step 6:

Spread remaining frosting over top and sides of cake. Chill for 15 minutes before serving.
